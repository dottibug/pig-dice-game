On your turn, roll the die as many times as you wish. Each roll gets added to your point total for the current round. Be careful—if you roll a 1, your points are lost and your turn is over!

If you think you've tested fate enough, click 'Hold Dice' to end your turn and add points from the current round to your game total. First player to reach 100 points wins!
